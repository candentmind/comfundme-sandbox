"We care for the most amazing marvelous people on Earth". This campaign is aim at supporting the health of the newborns, children, pregnant women and age people in Akweya land. The campaign, is one among many of the Deoch Concepts campaign. Supporting Oheta Clinic rehabilitation will help in improving the health of the newborn children, pregnant women, people and the age parents in Akweya land and the neighboring tribes too. The abandonment of Oheta Clinic, has cause injury and illness that has destroy the good health and development of our children, women and our age parents in turn affected the well-being of our community and has also increase in the dead rate. People now depends no self medication, because they cannot money to travel kilometres and miles to the city to access good medical care and diagnosis. As a Non-Profit, we rely on your generosity to help us continue to re-imagine a better health care in Akweya Land and the rehabilitation of Oheta Clinic. Your donation and supports of a good clinical equipment today will go a very long way in the treatment of people, caring for pregnant women and children who are the future leaders of our Akweya tomorrow, and our age parents who are the bank of our Akweya Knowledge, Culture, History and Heritage. Thank you for your support, and for being there for the most amazing people on earth in Akweya Land. This fundraising campaign is targeted at supporting OHETA CLINIC of Allan Akpa in Otukpo Local Government Area of Benue state.
