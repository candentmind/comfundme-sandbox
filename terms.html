2.1.5 There is no limit on the Funding Target you select for each Campaign.

2.1.6 When submitting details of your Project, you will be presented with three funding options, as follows:

  • ‘All Or Nothing’ – if you choose this option when listing your Project, we will only distribute funds to you if you reach your Fundraising Target. If you do not reach your Fundraising Target, all Pledges and tips will be returned to Donors.
  • ‘Sweep What Is Raised’– if you choose this option when listing your Project, we will distribute funds to you at the end of the Fundraising Period regardless whether any Fundraising Target has been reached. This option ,ay also be used for periodic sweeps.
  • ‘Forever Funding’– if you choose this option your Project will stay open to receive Pledges on a continuous basis (but at a minimum for the Fundraising Period below) and we will transfer funds to you on an agreed periodic basis. 

2.1.7 The minimum Fundraising Period that you can choose for your Project is 28 days and the maximum is 12 months.

You will not be able to resume that Project at a later date whilst retaining any of the Pledges you had received at the time of withdrawal.
